The prevalence of "new crown PTSD" is as high as 13%

After the stress period has passed, psychological problems may become the "second curve" of the epidemic. Recently, a new study published in "PLOS ONE" surveyed 1,040 participants in 5 countries. The results showed that more than 13% of people have symptoms of post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) related to the epidemic, and symptoms Reached the level of clinical diagnosis.
